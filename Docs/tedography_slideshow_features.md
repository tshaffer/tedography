# Tedography — First-Class Slideshow Feature Spec

# In first release:

## Playback Controls
- Play / Pause
- Next / Previous (manual navigation)
- Skip to first / last
- Adjustable speed (e.g., 2s, 5s, 10s per slide)
- Loop / no loop toggle

## Display & Layout
- Full-screen mode: maintain aspect ratio of photos
- Optional photo info overlay (title, date, location, people, keywords)
- Overlay fade-in on hover so it doesn't distract
- Progress bar showing position in the slideshow

## Ordering & Curation
- Play from current album or smart album
- Shuffle mode

## Interaction During Playback
- Click a photo to pause and see details
- Keyboard shortcuts (space = pause, arrows = next/prev, F = fullscreen, S = shuffle)

## Nice-to-Haves
- Captions / titles that animate in
- Date/location display pulled from EXIF data

# Not in first release:

## Transitions
- Fade, crossfade, slide, zoom, Ken Burns effect (slow pan/zoom — very cinematic)
- Selectable transition style and duration

## Display & Layout
- Fit vs. fill (letterbox vs. crop to fill screen)

## Ordering & Curation
- Date-based ordering (ascending/descending)
- Ability to exclude certain photos mid-show (mark as rejected)
- Filter by rating, keyword, or people before starting

## Audio
- Background music support (local file or playlist)
- Volume control
- Music that fades out when slideshow ends

## Sharing & Export
- Export slideshow as a video file (MP4)
- Generate a shareable link for a specific album slideshow
- Print/export a contact sheet of the displayed photos

## Interaction During Playback
- Star / rate a photo without leaving the slideshow
- Flag a photo as a favorite

## Nice-to-Haves
- "On this day" mode — shows photos from the same calendar date across all years
- Screensaver mode (activates after idle time)
