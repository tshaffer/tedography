    A few notes on how I normalized things:

* I changed things like Lori Birthday, Sam Birthday Celebration, and Joel Birthday Party into the reusable event keyword Birthday. The specific who belongs in People; the specific occasion can stay in albums or captions/notes.  
* I changed Jorgan Wedding, Manchester Wedding, and Wedding into the reusable event keyword Wedding. The specific wedding identity should usually be represented by album structure, optional event title metadata, or a collection/album, not separate near-duplicate keywords.  
* I kept Cozumel, Cabo, Baja, Maui, Yosemite, Monterey, Seattle, and similar labels only once, under Places, even though they recur in many albums across years.  
* I did not include Europe, Australia, PNW Trip, Southwest, Northeast, or year folders as both organizational and semantic keywords. Under a uniqueness constraint, those quickly become messy if used for multiple meanings.

The biggest principle I’d use going forward is:

* albums answer: “which trip / which event / which year / which curated set?”
* keywords answer: “who / where / what / what kind of thing?”

Because your album tree is heavily organized by year and trip/event containers, the best keyword tree should stay cross-cutting and reusable rather than trying to mirror the album hierarchy.  

The one thing in my rewritten tree I would still consider optional is whether broad regions like Europe and North America should exist as assignable keywords at all, versus being parent-only organizational nodes. My leaning is:

* allow them in the tree
* but usually assign the most specific child keyword available

So assign Paris or Spain, not just Europe, unless the exact country/city is unknown.

Mapping principles

* Geographic album labels usually map to one Place keyword.
* Event albums usually map to one Event keyword, often plus one or more People keywords.
* Subject albums usually map to one Subject keyword.
* Activity albums usually map to one Activity keyword.
* Operational / curation albums like Unfiled, Miscellany, Unknown Date, Imports from Takeout, dated import buckets, and source-bucket albums should usually map to no normal keywords.
